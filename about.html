Our Story

Cotton On, founded in 1991 in Geelong, Australia. It was established by Nigel Austin and his wife, Mariann. The first store was the modest market stall that sold denim jeans and basic clothing items. Despite a slow start, the business quickly gained traction and by 1995, it expanded to include mend and children’s clothing, as well as homewares. The company’s focus on providing affordable, on-trend fashion as remained central to its identity. The company launched several brands including Cotton On kids in 2004, Cotton On Body, Factorie, and Typo in 2007, and Rubi in 2008. As of 2020, Cotton On operates over 1500 stores in 18 countries, employing around 22000 people worldwide. The company has also made significant strides in sustainability, with initiatives like the Cotton On Foundation, which supports education and healthcare programs in disadvantaged communities.
